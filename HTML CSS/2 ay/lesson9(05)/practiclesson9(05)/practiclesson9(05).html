ABOUT US

Venturastic Fund’s is a Special Purpose Investment Holding Company (SPIC) formed with an impetus for creating, building business, & Merger and Acquisition.

It will aim at developing itself as a strong global player, by building a proven track record and achieving significant milestones in the next 5 years.

We will create and target business that have imperceptible potential for revenue growth, defensible intellectual property and strong market positions within their respective industries. Investing in large capitalization business with opportunities that will generate predictable cash flows and a compelling value proposition, we also want to contribute more to the economic growth in the countries where we expand our footprints while also promoting sustainable business practices, leveraging the power of many to deliver value to both mature and emerging business.

Our aim will be to build cash capital that will serves as a solid foundation for growth, facilitating expansion through business partnerships, in addition to this, Venturastic will also maintain close relationships with a number of domestic and overseas financial institutions.

Driven by a philosophy that seeks to add value to stakeholders, Venturastic will successfully engage in several major business ventures and business portfolios, ranging from Information, Communication & Technology, Media Broadcast, Fashion and luxury, this will form the core of its economy.

Corporate Social Responsibility

We sponsor Patient Capital and aim to back leaders from the marginalized communities who display determination, skills, imagination and understanding of what it takes to create change. Our program is designed to connect and cultivate a pipeline of entrepreneurs both Micro and Social who are committed to tackling poverty and injustice in their communities.

Patient capital is not a grant, it is an investment which is intended to recoup its principal. It would not seek to maximize financial returns on its investment, as we seek to maximize social impact, to capitalize the creation of markets to combat poverty and give an opportunity for growth to both Micro and Social enterprise.

With our patient capital program, we as the investor are willing to make a financial investment in a business with no expectation of turning a quick profit, instead investors will demand accountability in the form of return on capital and substantiated proof that the enterprise can grow sustainably in the long run. If we do have a very huge success story to share, we as a team would feel proud of being a part of that success.

Each year we aim to invest in 2 projects as a Patient Capital Investor and back these projects with our expertise, business advisory and strategic support, to ensure that they succeed and become self-sustainable.

We don’t just give the capital, but back the projects with our knowledge and experience.
