INVESTMENT INSIGHTS

Venturastic aims to be a leading investment firm investing in both greenfield projects and M&A in both enterprise and startup companies that offer growth prospects and add significant value. 

Business we invest in

We will invest in thoughtful founders and leadership teams.

We are entrepreneurs, C-Suite and board members ourselves and have managed teams of 20 up to 5,000. We have had our share of failures and success. We understand founders, management and they get us too.

We will invest in leaders who have built an internal conviction through experience, reflection and identified gaps, opportunity because they see things differently from most other people.

Businesses we believe in 

We will invest in scalable, capital-efficient, sustainable businesses that aim to create significant value than existing options.

We like gritty, straight shooters who have a bias for execution and learning, and are interested in creating valuable companies.

What Entrepreneurs and Business can expect?

We bring in accomplished advisors and management.

We believe business is not just about the solution generated or service given.

For any long-term success, the internal abilities of the business need to grow with market share attained.

We bring in accomplished advisors and management from our network appending fresh perspective to the business we partner and invest in, offering founders and companies a full suite of capital partnership, from cash to advice and mentorship.

Our delivery team is a congregation of talented founders, C-Suites, Investors, Strategy Leaders, Restructuring executives, SME executives, Human Capital, Data Scientist and AI leaders.
